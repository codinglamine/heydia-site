Features

Not a chatbot.
A second brain that does the work.

heydia connects to your real tools and acts across them, reasoning, remembering, and getting things done instead of just answering.

Chat & agent

Ask in plain words. heydia reasons, searches the web with citations, and takes action across your apps, not just a reply.

Mail & calendar

Your day at a glance, important mail surfaced and summarised, events and invites handled, with all-time search across inbox and calendar.

Meetings → notes

Record in person or on a call. heydia transcribes with speaker labels and writes clean, titled notes with decisions and action items.

Priorities, sorted

Tasks auto-organised into the Eisenhower matrix every morning, do, plan, delegate, drop, so you always know what matters now.

Real memory

heydia remembers who you are and what you're working toward, so you never re-explain yourself.

Connect everything

Spotify, Notion, Slack, GitHub, Docs, TickTick and more, one assistant across all of them, plus request any we don't have.
