How do I install heydia?
Grab the Windows download from the home page and run the installer. macOS and mobile are coming soon. You can also use heydia in your browser at heydia.onrender.com.
How do I connect Google, Spotify or Slack?
Open heydia → the profile icon → Connections, and hit Connect next to each app. It opens your browser to sign in, then syncs automatically. Some need a one-time setup key; heydia tells you exactly what's missing if so.
Do I need my own AI key?
heydia runs on a bring-your-own-key model, so your prompts go straight to your provider and you only pay for what you use, with no markup. Paste an Anthropic key during setup or later in Settings. Your key stays on your device.
Where is my data stored?
heydia is local-first: your notes, tasks and chats live on your own machine. When you use the cloud build, data syncs to your private account so it follows you across devices. It's never sold or used to train models, and you can export or delete it any time.
How do I cancel or change my plan?
Manage your plan from Settings → Account, or email us and we'll sort it the same day. Cancelling stops future billing; you keep access until the period ends.
