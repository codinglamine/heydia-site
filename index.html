Home

A home that reads you the day

Open heydia and you don't get a blank box, you get a read on your whole day. A spoken-style morning brief stitches together your weather, your calendar, how many tasks are flagged urgent, your next meeting, even the F1 weekend or the headlines you actually follow, and lays it all out in one calm glance.

The orb sits at the centre. Tap it and just talk, or type a question and watch it pull the answer from across your connected apps.

  • A morning brief written for you, in plain language
  • Weather, events, tasks, music and news in one place
  • Tap the orb to talk, type to ask anything
heydia / Home
Chat & agent

Ask once. It does the work.

This is not a chatbot that stops at advice. Ask heydia in plain words and it reasons, searches the web with real citations, then acts across the tools you've connected: sending the email, moving the event, creating the playlist, opening the page.

Every chat remembers what you're working on, so you never start from zero, and a temporary mode lets you ask something off the record, with nothing saved to history or memory.

  • Real actions across your apps, not just replies
  • Web search with citations you can check
  • Memory across chats, plus a private temporary mode
heydia / Chat
Priorities

Your day, triaged before you wake up

Every morning heydia sorts your tasks into the Eisenhower matrix, do it now, plan it, delegate, drop it, so the first thing you see is what actually matters, not a flat to-do list a mile long.

Your meetings, habits and deadlines sit right alongside it, and one tap re-runs the triage whenever the day changes. Drag anything between quadrants and it stays put.

  • Auto-triage into the four quadrants every morning
  • Meetings, habits and tasks on one day view
  • Drag and drop to reprioritise in a second
heydia / Day
Weather

Weather you can plan around

Not just a number. heydia gives you the feels-like temperature, an hour-by-hour strip you can scrub through, a smooth 24-hour curve, and a seven-day outlook you can open day by day.

When a heatwave or a storm is on the way it surfaces a plain-language alert at the top, so you know to grab a jacket or stay hydrated before you ever walk out the door.

  • Hourly strip and a scrubbable 24-hour curve
  • Seven-day forecast, expandable per day
  • Plain-language heat and storm alerts
heydia / Weather
Meetings & notes

Meetings turn into notes by themselves

Hit record for an in-person conversation or a call and heydia transcribes it, then writes clean, titled notes with the decisions and action items pulled out for you.

The editor is proper rich text, bold, highlights, colours, headings, pins, and you and heydia can both write in the same note. Everything syncs to Google Docs, so your work lives where your team already looks.

  • Records in person or on a call, then writes it up
  • Titled notes with decisions and action items
  • Rich editor with Google Docs sync
heydia / Notes
Messages

One inbox for everything

Mail, Slack and your chats land in a single, searchable place, so you stop bouncing between five tabs to find one thread. heydia surfaces what's important, summarises the noisy ones, and lets you act, reply, send, archive, without leaving the window.

Search runs across all of it at once, with all-time history, so the thing you half-remember from three months ago is one query away.

  • Mail, Slack and chats in one unified inbox
  • Important surfaced, the noisy ones summarised
  • One search across everything, all-time
heydia / Messages
Entertainment

Your music and your race weekend

heydia drives Spotify directly, search, play, queue, pin the playlists you actually use, and control the volume without breaking your flow. YouTube and more sit in the same place.

It's race-week aware too: when a Grand Prix is coming it flips on an F1 theme, counts down to lights out, and drops a watch-live button right where you are, with the full driver and team standings a tap away.

  • Full Spotify control with pinned playlists
  • Race-week F1 mode with a live countdown
  • Standings, calendar and YouTube built in
heydia / Entertainment
Themes & customization

Make it yours

heydia isn't one-size-fits-all. Switch between a calm light theme and a deep dark one, flip on the F1 theme on race weekend, and tune the assistant's persona, the layout, and which widgets you even see.

Plus and Pro unlock the full set, every theme, custom personas, and a home dashboard you arrange yourself, so the app ends up feeling like it was built for exactly the way you work.

  • Light, dark and seasonal themes
  • Custom persona, layout and widgets
