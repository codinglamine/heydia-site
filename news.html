Changelog

What's new

Every heydia update in one place. We ship constantly, here's the story so far.

Jun 2026
v0.5 · Focus

Screen time, limits & locking in

A whole Screen Time tab, inspired by the best focus apps. heydia now tracks where your day on screen actually goes and helps you steer it.

  • New Screen time tab: focus ratio, category breakdown, 7-day trend, AI insights.
  • Per-app daily limits — cross one and heydia shows a full-screen "lock in" prompt instead of nagging.
  • Mark any app as distraction or deep work, and link an app to a goal to see the time it earns.
  • Reminders that fire as real notifications: hourly water, meeting prep, and a daily screen-time digest.
  • Browser extension can now block distracting sites with a calm "lock in" page.
Jun 2026
v0.4 · Sync & Goals

Your day, on every device

Sign in anywhere and pick up exactly where you left off — no re-entering anything.

  • Cloud sync: chats, tasks, notes, goals and settings now follow you across the desktop app and the web.
  • Goals, reimagined: one widget with daily / weekly / monthly / yearly horizons, progress, sub-tasks, and AI "suggest goals for me".
  • Tap a task in the priority matrix to break it into steps with AI.
  • Folders moved into Notes and can hold links that open in heydia.
Jun 2026
v0.3 · Design

The onyx redesign + liquid glass

A calmer, flatter, more premium heydia — and a brand-new identity.

  • Flat onyx dark theme as the default: readable, minimal, no murk. Now with a proper light mode too.
  • A liquid-glass navigation selector that springs between tabs.
  • The heydia mark: a clean squircle whose dot follows your cursor (toggleable).
  • Granola-style notifications and a tidier, evened-out home grid.
Jun 2026
v0.2 · Connectors

Plug in your whole world

heydia learned to reach across the apps you already live in.

  • @-mention chats, notes, connected apps, and people right in any chat.
  • An in-app web viewer, an F1 corner with live news, and a richer briefing.
  • One-click connectors for Gmail, Calendar, Notion, Slack, GitHub, Spotify and more.
