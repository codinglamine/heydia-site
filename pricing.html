Questions

Good to know

Why do I bring my own AI key?+

So your AI usage goes through your own provider account at cost, no markup, no surprise bill from us, and your prompts never sit on our servers. You grab a key from Anthropic in a minute and paste it once.

How do connectors work?+

Each integration is a one-click secure sign-in (OAuth) with the service. heydia only gets the access you grant, and you can revoke any connection instantly. Every plan unlocks all of them.

What are the token limits?+

Each plan includes a monthly pool of heydia AI, Starter 2M tokens (≈1,500 chats), Pro 10M (≈7,500 chats), Unlimited has no cap. A typical chat is a few thousand tokens, so most days you won't think about it. Hit the cap and you can top up, upgrade, or just add your own AI key, which makes usage uncapped on any plan.

What's the difference between Starter and Pro?+

Starter is the full assistant for daily life, all connectors, briefing, voice, tasks. Pro adds 5× the tokens plus power-user modes: deep research, voice conversations, switching between AI models, the meeting recorder, and unlimited automations.

Is my data private?+

Yes. heydia is local-first in spirit, your conversations and connected accounts are yours, never sold, never used to train models, and exportable or deletable anytime.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Anytime, in one click, from your account. You keep access until the end of your billing period, and your data stays yours.
