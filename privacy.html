Privacy Policy

Last updated 27 June 2026 · Effective immediately for all users.

heydia ("heydia", "we", "us") builds a personal assistant that helps you run your day. This policy explains what we collect, why, how we protect it, and the control you have. We wrote it to be read, not skipped. If anything is unclear, email dia.customersupport@gmail.com.

The short version: your data is yours. We use it only to make heydia work for you. We never sell it, never show you ads, and never use your content to train AI models. You can export or delete everything at any time.

1. Who is responsible for your data

heydia is the data controller for the account and content data described below. For questions or to exercise your rights, contact dia.customersupport@gmail.com.

2. What we collect

  • Account details — your email, the name you give us, and (optionally) profile details like your city, role, and preferences you enter during onboarding.
  • Content you create — conversations with heydia, notes, tasks, goals, folders, and similar data you make inside the app.
  • Connected-account access — when you connect a service (Google, Microsoft/Outlook, Notion, Slack, Spotify, GitHub, TickTick, and others), we store the access tokens that service grants, so heydia can act on your behalf for the things you ask. You can revoke any connection at any time.
  • Your AI key — if you bring your own AI key, we store it encrypted and use it only to make the AI requests you trigger.
  • Screen-time data (desktop, optional) — if you use automatic time tracking, the app samples which application is in focus to build your screen-time view. This is processed and stored on your device by default; it only leaves your device if you have cloud sync enabled.
  • Technical data — basic, privacy-respecting logs needed to run the service securely (e.g. error logs, approximate region for weather/time). We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

3. How we use it

Only to provide and improve heydia for you: to run the assistant, sync your data across your devices, perform the actions you request on connected apps, secure your account, process payments, and respond to support. That's the whole list.

4. Legal bases (EU/UK users, GDPR)

We process your data to perform our contract with you (providing the service you signed up for), on the basis of your consent (for connecting third-party accounts and optional features like screen-time tracking, which you can withdraw anytime), and for our legitimate interests in keeping the service secure and working. We process payment data to comply with legal obligations.

5. AI processing

When you ask heydia something, the relevant context is sent to your chosen AI provider (e.g. Anthropic) using your key or a heydia-provided allowance, solely to generate your answer. Your prompts and content are not used to train any model, and we do not sell or share them. Your provider processes the request under its own terms.

6. Who we share data with (sub-processors)

We don't sell your data or share it with advertisers. We use a small set of trusted providers strictly to run heydia:

  • Supabase — authentication and encrypted database storage.
  • Render — application hosting.
  • Stripe — payment processing (we never see your full card details).
  • Your AI provider (e.g. Anthropic) and the apps you connect — data flows to these only as needed to perform actions you request, under your own accounts.

7. International transfers

Some providers above operate outside your country (including the United States). Where required, transfers rely on appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. How long we keep it

We keep your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your associated content from our systems promptly (within 30 days), except where we must keep limited records to meet legal or accounting obligations.

9. Security

Access is protected by authentication and row-level security so you can only ever reach your own data. AI keys and OAuth tokens are stored encrypted. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and will notify you of a breach affecting you as required by law.

10. Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data, and disconnect any integration, at any time from Settings. EU/UK users also have the rights to restrict or object to processing, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Email us to exercise any right.

11. Children

heydia is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where required by local law). We don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we'll remove it.

12. Cookies & local storage

We use only the storage needed to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. No advertising or cross-site tracking.

13. Changes

If we make a material change, we'll update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app or by email.

This document is written to be clear and complete, but it is not legal advice. Before public launch heydia will have it reviewed by a qualified professional for your jurisdiction.
